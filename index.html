Singapore Tourism Insights: A Multifaceted Journey

Introduction

On this page, you can see some data visualization of Singapore Tourism.
1. The number of international visitors traveling to Singapore has been increasing steadily over the last few decades until 2019 where the COVID-19 pandemic hits the world. The tourists has dropped tremendously for 2 years and started to recover in year 2022. This can be shown clearly on the line chart on the right.
2. On the far right, the Choropleth map show the quantity of international visitors by country arriving Singapore from Aug 2022 to Jul 2023.
3. The chart below show the breakdown of international visitors by region.
4. The scatter plot show the relationship between tourists and hotel occupancy rate.
5. The heatmap shows the trend of Singaporeans travel overseas. It is noted that Singapore travelling mostly in Mar, Jun, Nov and Dec. Note: The number of international tourists and Singaporeans travel overseas in the data only include those by Air and Sea, not including those via land.
